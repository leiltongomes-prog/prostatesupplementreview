What the Doctors Don't Tell You About BPH

Here's something I discovered after months of research that genuinely shocked me: there's growing evidence that fungal inflammation may be a significant — and overlooked — driver of prostate enlargement. The warm, moist environment of the prostate and urinary tract creates the perfect conditions for certain fungi to thrive.

When this fungal overgrowth triggers chronic inflammation, the prostate swells. And that swelling is what causes the weak stream, the urgency, the incomplete emptying — all of it.

Prescription alpha-blockers like Flomax treat the symptoms. They relax the muscle around the urethra. But they don't address the underlying inflammation. Which is exactly why so many men find the relief temporary — or non-existent.
